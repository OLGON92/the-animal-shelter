Welcome to Chet's animal shelter. Our mission started when our founder Chet Elderson found a stray cat named Josiah on the side of the road. At first he wasn't a fan of animals at all, but Josiah helped him change his mind. Ever since then Chet has been running his own animal shelter.

Shadow the Wonder Cat

Shadow the Wonder Cat is a lovely little red-headed tabby that was found in a storm drain. He is called the Wonder Cat because of his incredible jumping ability. He his incredibly firendly; especially with children. He will make a fine addition to any family!

Derek the Amazing Beagle

Derek the Amazing Beagle is medium-sized brown beagle that joined our shleter after being abandoned in a box behind the local mini-mart. He is Amazing because of his keen sense of smell and because of the almost einstein-like level of intelligence he posseses. He absolutely loves being around people, other dogs, and children. Adopt him while you can!

Domino the Super Pony

Domino the Super Pony is a black and white pony, hence the name Domino, that came into our shelter after her original owner could no-longer care for her. She is Super fast and agile. She absolutely loves the occasional sugar-cube or two. She loves all types of people and could use a good home.
